About

Hi, I'm Claudia.

I do what I love — I build websites, shoot videos, and create visual content that helps small and large brands shine online.

I started with websites, but quickly realized most clients have the same problem: they have a site, but no content to bring people in. So today I offer everything in one place — a website, video for social media, and AI-generated visuals that match your brand.

My clients are very diverse — from fastener and hardware companies, ski schools, construction firms, restaurants, all the way to pharmacy. Every industry speaks its own language, and my job is to understand it.

"I don't make what I find beautiful. I make what actually works for you."
