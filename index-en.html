About

Hi, I'm Claudia.

I do what I love — I build websites, shoot videos, and create visual content that helps small and bigger brands shine online.

I started with websites, but quickly noticed most clients had the same problem: they had a site but no content to drive traffic to it. So today I offer everything in one place — a website, video for social media, and AI-generated visuals that match your brand.

My clients are very diverse — from a fastener-materials company, a ski school, construction firms, restaurants, all the way to pharma. Every industry speaks its own language, and my job is to understand it.

"I don't make what I think looks nice. I make what actually works for you."
